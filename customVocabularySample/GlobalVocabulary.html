Rosenwald Fund Collection Vocabulary

This vocabulary defines classes and properties for organizing data about Rosenwald Fund materials.

Namespace URI

The namespace URI for this vocabulary is: https://rosenwald.fisk.edu/GlobalVocabulary#

Classes

  • rfc:Rosenwald Fund Collection: The Rosenwald Fund Collection.

Properties

  • rfc:schoolName: The official name(s) of the school.
  • rfc:alternateSchoolNames: Any alternate names or variations used for the school.
  • rfc:county: The county where the school is located.
  • rfc:state: The state where the school is located.
  • rfc:yearOfEstablishment: The year the school was established.
  • rfc:acreage: The total land area of the school property, measured in acres.
  • rfc:latitude: The geographic latitude of the school.
  • rfc:longitude: The geographic longitude of the school.
  • rfc:applicationNumber: Indexing of the numerical application that led to funding for the school.
  • rfc:itemNumber: Archival indexing of the fund card.
  • rfc:teacherType: Number of teachers the school had.
  • rfc:totalCost: Total cost of funding the school.
  • rfc:negroesContribution: Total funding from the local African American community.
  • rfc:whitesContribution: Total funding from the local European American community.
  • rfc:publicContribution: Total funding from the public.
  • rfc:rosenwaldContribution: Total funding from the Julius Rosenwald Fund.
  • rfc:demographic: Race or ethnicity of aid applicant.
